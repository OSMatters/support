FAQ

Q: How do I start a practice session?
A: Simply open the app, choose a daily reading or system material, and follow the guided steps to listen, practice, and review.

Q: Do I need an account to use OralEase?
A: No account or login is required. You can start practicing immediately after downloading the app.

Q: How do I report an error or suggest improvements?
A: Please send us an email with details. We appreciate your feedback and will continue improving the app.
